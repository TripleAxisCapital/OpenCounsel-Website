FAQ

Does it require the internet?
No. By default, all inference and retrieval are local. Optional connectors can be toggled off.
What models can I use?
Any GGUF model compatible with llama.cpp. Choose sizes based on hardware.
How is data stored?
Encrypted workspace on disk with export/import. Embeddings are local via ChromaDB.
