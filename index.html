OC Core System

Modular intelligence, built for real work.

Each Core is a focused, updatable capability—research, coaching, drafting, and more—running inside the private OC workspace. Mix and match cores to fit the task, then ship with confidence.

OC-Core

Orchestration • Local-first • Routing

The engine of OC. Routes prompts, tools, and databases to the right models—securely, privately, and fast. Enables memory, profiles, and audit-friendly outputs.

  • Model autodiscovery & smart routing
  • Local-first execution & sandboxing
  • Prompt/Tool graphs with guardrails
Included

OC-Research-Core

RAG • Citations • Source control

Curated databases + verifiable search. Pull papers, cases, and PDFs with inline citations and conflict checks—so you can trust the output.

  • Cite-as-you-go answers
  • PDF ingestion & semantic search
  • Cross-source consensus & filters
Included

  • Action plans & feedback loops
  • Memory-aware tutoring
  • Voice-ready session flow
