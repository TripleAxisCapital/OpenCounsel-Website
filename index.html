OC Cores

Meet the Cores

OC-Core, OC-Research-Core, and OC-Coach-Core work together like a product family. Each Core is focused, updatable, and designed to feel native on your device. New Cores ship regularly—and there’s a dedicated to explore them all.

OC-Core

Your default brain: general reasoning, planning, and drafting powered by OC’s local stack.

  • Fast on-device context & structured outputs.
  • Writes briefs, emails, and docs with export-ready formatting.
  • Private by design — no cloud send.

OC-Research-Core

Verifiable research: crawls sources, ranks evidence, and returns linked, deduped summaries.

  • Quote capture with line-level references.
  • Evidence ranking & conflict resolution.
  • One-tap audit trail to originals.

OC-Coach-Core

Structured coaching for goals, habits, and decisions — with weekly reviews and action plans.

  • Clear objectives, milestones, and checkpoints.
  • Progress feedback loops and nudges.
  • Private, local-first coaching data.
